The Format in One Paragraph

					
Lives at /llms.txt, uses Markdown, includes an H1 with your site name, optional blockquote summary, and H2 sections grouping URLs with human-readable descriptions. Think of it as a curated table of contents for AI.

				

			

			

				
üîÑ

				

					
How It Differs From robots.txt & Sitemaps

					
robots.txt controls access (what crawlers may NOT fetch). sitemap.xml supports discovery (lists all URLs). llms.txt is about curation‚Äîhighlighting the most useful resources for LLMs in a human-structured way.

				

			

			

				
üìà

				

					
Current Adoption Reality

					
~10% penetration in large samples. Heavy concentration in AI tooling, API docs, and developer SaaS. Major consumer sites (Google, Meta, Amazon) show near-zero adoption. It's mostly a developer and SEO tool phenomenon‚Äînot yet a mainstream standard.

The Pragmatic Take

					
Don't treat it as a must-have ranking factor. DO treat it as a low-effort experiment where tooling exists. A place to practice GEO thinking: curating your most important content for AI consumption.
