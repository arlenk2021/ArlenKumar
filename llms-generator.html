üìÑ What is llms.txt?

llms.txt is a proposed standard (similar to robots.txt) that helps Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini understand your website's structure and content. It provides AI-readable documentation that improves how AI systems cite and reference your content for better Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).
