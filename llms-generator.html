What is llms.txt?

A human-curated Markdown file listing your most important pages so LLMs can understand your content at inference time.

The Format in One Paragraph

					
Lives at /llms.txt, uses Markdown, includes an H1 with your site name, optional blockquote summary, and H2 sections grouping URLs with descriptions.

				

			

			

				

					
				

				

					
How It Differs From robots.txt & Sitemaps

					
robots.txt controls access. sitemap.xml supports discovery. llms.txt is about curation—highlighting resources for LLMs.

				

			

			

				

					
				

				

					
Current Adoption Reality

					
~10% penetration in large samples. Heavy in AI tooling and developer SaaS. It's a developer and SEO phenomenon—not yet mainstream.
